    • About Us

    S Mart is an online marketplace that brings the supermarket to your doorstep by listing your favourite local shops online and delivering on time every time.The main objective for S Mart is customer satisfaction.
    Now you can order your products online from the comfort of your home and leave the quality checking, careful packing and on time delivery to us.

    How it Works?

    S Mart is an enabling platform for all your local retail shops. We provide great opportunities to the retail shops by expanding their reach, increasing their sales and marketing their brand.
    We help them list their products online on our platform and handle the orders, payments, pickup and delivery.
    This gives a competitive advantage to our vendors over their competitors instantly. As for the customers, S Mart provides the freedom of choice, option to compare products from different vendors, best price and
    most importantly the comfort of ordering from home.

    Why S Mart?

    S Mart is started by a young, passionate and technology savvy group of entrepreneurs from Thanjavur.We are committed to bring only the best to you in terms of quality, price and variety.The founders have traveled
    the world quite extensively, have vast experience in the retail, wholesale, trading, ecommerce and mobile technologies and hope to implement the best practices from different countries to benefit their homeland
    where it all began.
